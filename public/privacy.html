Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026  |  Last updated: April 8, 2026

NYTHOS ("we", "us", "our") operates the platform at nythos.io. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

We may collect the following when you use NYTHOS:

  • Wallet address — when you connect your wallet or join the presale.
  • Email address — if you submit the founder list or presale signup form.
  • Referral codes — captured from URL parameters to credit referrers.
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, timestamps (collected via analytics if enabled).
  • Technical data — browser type, device type, IP address, referring URL.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and improve the NYTHOS platform.
  • To process presale participation and token allocations.
  • To send product updates and launch communications (email opt-in only).
  • To detect fraud and ensure platform security.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Blockchain Data

Wallet addresses and on-chain transactions are public by nature of blockchain technology. We do not control or store on-chain data — it is publicly visible on the Base network. Do not share sensitive information on-chain expecting privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers — hosting, analytics, email delivery (under data processing agreements).
  • Legal authorities — when required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Successors — in the event of a merger or acquisition.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use a cookie to store your consent preference for this notice. If analytics are enabled, cookies or local storage may be used to measure usage. You can clear browser cookies at any time. See our cookie notice at the bottom of the site.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address until you unsubscribe. Wallet address associations are kept as long as necessary to honor presale commitments. You can request deletion by emailing us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent).

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@nythos.io.

8. GDPR (EU/UK Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, our legal bases for processing are: your consent (email sign-up), contract performance (presale), and legitimate interests (platform security and analytics). You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Children

NYTHOS is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will post the new version here with a revised effective date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Questions? Email us at hello@nythos.io.
