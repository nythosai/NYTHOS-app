## Product Snapshot

NYTHOS is a Base first onchain intelligence platform.
It helps traders, builders, communities, and small desks monitor what matters across:

- live signals
- launch radar
- smart wallet tracking
- bridge and DEX flow
- private alerts
- saved views
- proof and accuracy
- API access

## Brand Positioning

NYTHOS should feel:

- premium
- credible
- sharp
- operator focused
- futuristic without looking fake

NYTHOS should not feel:

- scammy
- loud
- casino like
- meme driven
- public sale obsessed

## Core Narrative

The best crypto products do not win by showing more data.
They win by helping users trust what matters.

NYTHOS turns noisy onchain activity into cleaner intelligence for Base.
It gives users a better way to monitor wallet behavior, launch flow, alerts, and proof without drowning in clutter.
