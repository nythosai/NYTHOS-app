## What are you building?

NYTHOS is building a Base-first onchain intelligence product that converts raw blockchain activity into usable signals, monitored-wallet workflows, and API-ready insight for traders, builders, and communities.

## What problem are you solving?

Most crypto users still rely on a broken stack: raw explorers, delayed social commentary, or expensive institutional tools. NYTHOS closes that gap by making onchain activity easier to interpret and act on.

## Why are you building on Base?

Base is the best fit for NYTHOS because the contracts are already configured for Base, the wallet experience supports the planned access model, and Base offers real funding and distribution paths for builders who are shipping products instead of chasing hype.

## What stage is the project at?

Working beta. The frontend is live as a working build, the signal engine runs, the founder-list and referral flow are live, and the smart-contract suite is locally tested. The token contracts are not yet deployed.

## What proof exists today?

We have a working frontend beta, a live signal engine, Base-aligned contract configuration, founder-list capture, `108` local smart-contract tests passing, and a passing production frontend build.

## What will the grant help you do?

The grant will help us harden the product, improve demo and onboarding quality, prepare contracts for external review, and convert founder-list demand into pilots and Base launch readiness.

## How does this help the Base ecosystem?

NYTHOS gives Base a sharper intelligence layer. It helps traders, communities, and builders understand wallet and market activity faster, while creating a product that can later tie access and monetization directly to Base usage.

## Why now?

Onchain data is more available than ever, but interpretation is still too slow or too expensive for most users. Base also provides a strong environment for focused products to grow with funding and distribution support.
