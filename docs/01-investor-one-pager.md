## What NYTHOS is

NYTHOS is a working crypto intelligence product that turns noisy onchain activity into usable signals. It detects whale movements, scores significance, tracks watched wallets, and exposes that intelligence through a frontend, Telegram workflow, and API.

The product is already positioned around Base as the launch chain for the future access layer.

## Problem

Retail and small crypto teams are overwhelmed by raw blockchain data and lagging social signals.

Existing options are weak in three ways:

- Explorers show raw activity with no decision-ready interpretation
- Premium intelligence tools are too expensive for most users
- By the time signals spread on social media, the useful edge is gone

## Solution

NYTHOS packages onchain intelligence into a simpler workflow:

- Detect whale-sized activity across major chains
- Score and rank events by relevance
- Surface proof and history in a working dashboard
- Deliver intelligence through Telegram and API-ready flows
- Prepare a Base-native token access layer only after traction
