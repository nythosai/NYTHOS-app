## Problem

Most crypto users still depend on a bad stack:

- explorers for raw data
- social media for delayed interpretation
- expensive terminals for professional-quality intelligence

The result is that traders and small teams see the market too late or pay too much for signal quality.

## Solution

NYTHOS packages blockchain intelligence into a more usable product:

- detect meaningful whale activity
- score and rank events
- monitor watched wallets
- expose proof and history
- deliver through frontend, Telegram, and API workflows

## Suggested answers for application fields

### What are you building?

NYTHOS is a Base-first onchain intelligence product that converts raw blockchain activity into usable signals, watched-wallet workflows, and proof-based insight for traders, builders, and communities.

### Why are you building on Base?

Base gives NYTHOS the cleanest launch environment for the next stage of the product. The contracts are already configured for Base, the wallet UX fits the intended access model, and Base offers real builder funding and distribution paths that are stronger than trying to force a public token launch too early.

### What stage is the project at?

Working beta. The frontend ships, the signal engine runs, the founder-list and referral flow are live, and the contract suite is locally tested. The token contracts are not yet deployed.

### What are your current traction signals?

Our traction today is product proof rather than inflated vanity numbers: working beta, live signal engine, founder-list capture, Base-aligned contract setup, `108` passing smart-contract tests, and a passing production frontend build.

### What will this grant help you do?

This grant will help us harden the product, improve demo and onboarding quality, prepare the contract suite for external review, and convert early founder-list demand into real pilot users and Base launch readiness.

### How does NYTHOS help the Base ecosystem?

NYTHOS gives Base a sharper intelligence layer. It can help traders, builders, and communities understand wallet and market activity faster, while also creating a Base-native product that can later tie access and monetization directly to onchain usage.
